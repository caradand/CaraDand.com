TUNED

MUSIC MAGAZIN

Publication Design

OVERVIEW

Tuned is a magazine about music, life, culture and art with a plethora of sex appeal. Get Tuned with talent birthed from the local creatives of Western Canada. This bi-monthly publication provides readers the insider scoop on top musical acts and the creative, passionate sounds of the artists coming from and to the West Coast. Tuned's style is flashy, bold, edgy and designed to jump out and engage its readers.

DESIGN PROBLEM

The cover has strong contrast with engaging photos set against a stark white background. Tuned's feature articles are the magazine's strong point with bold, in your face visuals. Tuned takes risks in its approach to magazine editorial. Tuned will be distributed through controlled circulation at select locations including: music festivals, local record shops, local apparel retailers and musical instrument stores.
