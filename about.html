ABOUT ME!

Hello! I'm Cara. My background is in both marketing and design. Currently, I do creative work for a rad marketing agency in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. I love what I do creating and refreshing brands and brand media for an array of great clientele.

I possess a Bachelor of Commerce, Marketing with Distinction, from the Edwards School of Business, at the University of Saskatchewan, as well as, a Graphic Design Diploma from the Vancouver College of Art and Design.

Through the study of design my objective has been to supplement my marketing and communications knowledge with practical, hands-on design skills. In addition to the technical skills I have acquired, the program has driven my research and strategic decision-making abilities in order to produce effective communication solution results for the appropriate target audience.
