About Me

Hello! I'm Cara. I create visual brand identities and graphics at Rock & Bloom, a marketing and branding agency located in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. I love what I do creating and refreshing brands and brand media for an array of great clientele. My areas of expertise include branding, marketing strategy and communication design. My background is in both marketing and design.



I possess a Bachelor of Commerce, Marketing with Distinction, from the Edwards School of Business, at the University of Saskatchewan, as well as, a Graphic Design Diploma from the Vancouver College of Art and Design.
