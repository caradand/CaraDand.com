vangool wellness

Vangool Wellness required a refresh in their brand identity and web presence. The design challenge was to inject a non-traditional, adventurous approach to the brand. The symmetric design is geometricly appealing and offers a very open, almost bursting feel. The clean lines and circular outlining element is modern and stylish.
