Branding, Package Design

OVERVIEW

Dandy Tea provides a wonderful way to cozy up and treat yourself on a cold day. Tea boxes for three unique flavours were developed: Green, darjeeling and blueberry tea. Each box includes illustrations that epitomize the essence of each flavour. Green tea features a panda bear and green tea leaves. Darjeeling is reflective of India with luscious purple and elephants. Blueberry tea depicts Canadian roots and features geese, bears and moose. The new modern graphics will draw new and younger customers, making tea fun!
