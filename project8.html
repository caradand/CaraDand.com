THE KNACK

ART&CULTURE MAGAZINE

Publication Design

COMPANY OVERVIEW

The Knack magazine brings into focus the tasteful occurrences in contemporary art and culture. The primary focuses include art, design, photography, film, music and performance. It features people with "The Knack" - the creative, talented, intellectual and selfless souls deserving recognition and attention. Covering topics related to the arts within the context of culture and lifestyle that directly relates to the people with "The Knack."

DESIGN PROBLEM

The Knack will expose the up and coming talent and connect people to the passion of the west coast. The visual stimuli is bold and progressive. Graphics jump off the page creating delight and excitement as emotional selling points. Every page is artfully crafted with the discipline of design principles and layout as a strong foundation for this experimental, exciting editorial.
